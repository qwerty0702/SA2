Solutions to our current problem
One of the way of saving water would be - filtering the wasted water. Currently, the United Arab Emirates are Singapore are filtering the water and make it into drinkable water, and it is completely carbon free as well.
UAE that uses desallination from sea water and pollued water
Another effective solution for protecting the environment and reducing the amount of water pollution is to manage stormwater when possible. The storm picks up harmful pollutants into storm drains, streams and rivers. It can be treated and managed through a variety of different processes, which can turn into a drinkable water and also safe as well.
