Problems that we are facing now
One of the major problem that we are facing might be - the water pollution. The pollution of a water has brought many disadvantages, such as people cannot drink the fresh water anymore. Moreover, this can lead to a serious infection or viruses that leads people to death sometimes.
One of the polluted river in India
The another problem would be - the waste that industries and factories produce. The chemical wastes, that is being wasted to river or sea, causes a fatal damage to both animals and people. If someone drinks it, person might die and the crops that is grown with the chemical waste also becomes a problem, and reduces the amount of production of crops everytime.
